Detailed Description

struct prelim_drm_i915_gem_vm_bind

VA to object/buffer mapping to [un]bind.

NOTE: A vm_bind will hold a reference on the BO which is released during corresponding vm_unbind or while closing the VM. Hence closing the BO alone will not ensure BO is released.
